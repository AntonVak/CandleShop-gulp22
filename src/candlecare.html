CANDLE CARE

Our hand poured candles are designed to be a sculptural piece in your home, we use the highest quality cotton wicks that will ensure an even and long - lasting burn time.

Each candle is unique in the way it burns.

We recommend the following tips for burning your new candle:

FIRST BURN

Before lighting your candle, place it on a flat dish or candle plate that will collect any wax that may drip. (tear drip)

Light your candle in a well-ventilated safe space, clear of flowers and other flammable objects.

Let the candle burn for at least 1 x hour or so that a “melt pool” is created. Monitor the candle regularly.

Always remember to blow your candle out when you leave the room or go to bed.

ONGOING USE

When not in use, place your candle away from direct sunlight and nearby heat sources.

Before you light the candle again, we recommend that you trim the wick 2-3cm from the surface of the wax, to ensure a consistent smoke-free flame.

We recommend that you burn the candle for no more than 3 hours at a time.

CANDLE SAFETY

Keep your candles out of reach from children and pets. Especially curious fluffy dogs and cats.
